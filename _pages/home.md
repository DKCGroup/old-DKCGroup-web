We are a research group at [Tsinghua-Berkeley Shenzhen Institute](http://www.tbsi.edu.cn/english/), [Institute of Data and Information](https://www.sigs.tsinghua.edu.cn/en/Institute_of_Data_and_Information/list.htm), [Tsinghua Shenzhen International Graduate School](https://www.sigs.tsinghua.edu.cn/). [Our team](https://dkcgroup.github.io/team/) is led by Dr. Kaichen Dong [(Google Scholar)](https://scholar.google.cz/citations?hl=zh-CN&authuser=1&user=yBX-oTwAAAAJ).
